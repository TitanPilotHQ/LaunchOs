# Titan Pilot Brand Bible

## Mission
Make AI-assisted trading decisions provable, governable, and committee-ready.

## Category
**Supervised AI Trading**

## Positioning
Titan Pilot is the system of record for AI trading decisions.

## Brand promise
Every thesis, citation, objection, score, approval, refusal, and cost can be reconstructed from evidence.

## Personality
Precise, restrained, skeptical, transparent, calm, technically credible.

## Voice
Use short declarative sentences. State evidence, scope, and limitations. Prefer facts over adjectives.

Never use: guaranteed, profitable, winning, effortless, AI-powered profits, high-conviction marketing, get rich, one-click autonomy.

## Signature lines
- AI reasons. Software decides.
- Evidence or it did not happen.
- The default action is nothing.
- Trust by architecture.

## Visual principles
Dark-first. One instrument-blue accent. Violet only marks model testimony. Critical red is reserved for HALTED or failed integrity states. Numbers use tabular alignment. Motion settles and never bounces.

## Anti-gambling rules
No P&L confetti, streaks, flames, leaderboards, rolling numbers, countdown pressure, fake urgency, luxury imagery, or signal-firehose framing.

## Claim governance
Every public measurable claim requires a source, evidence date, owner, public-safe wording, and review date.

## Definition of done
A brand asset is complete only when it is consistent with the commercial strategy, approved messaging, visual tokens, claim register, and public-safety rules.
