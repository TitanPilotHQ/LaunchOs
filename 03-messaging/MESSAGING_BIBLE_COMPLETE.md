# Titan Pilot Messaging Bible

## One-line category statement
Titan Pilot is the system of record for AI trading decisions.

## Elevator pitch
Your traders already use AI. Titan Pilot records every thesis, citation, objection, score, approval, refusal, and cost as replayable evidence. It does not promise returns; it gives the desk control and proof.

## Buyer-specific messages
### Head of Trading
Govern AI adoption without slowing the desk or pretending it is not already happening.

### Risk Officer
Inspect the exact reasoning, counter-arguments, deterministic controls, approvals, and exceptions behind every decision.

### Compliance / Committee
Replace a policy-only answer with an enforced, replayable system.

### Technical buyer
One event-sourced decision ledger, strict model contracts, deterministic scoring, provider abstraction, and replay verification.

### Investor
A system-of-record company in a category full of unprovable return claims.

## Message hierarchy
1. Your desk already uses AI.
2. The current usage is difficult to govern or defend.
3. Titan creates an evidence record for every decision.
4. AI remains advisory; deterministic software and humans retain authority.
5. The record is replayable and auditable.

## Proof points
- Certified shadow operation.
- Machine-validated citations.
- Adversarial review.
- Deterministic score components.
- Hard pre-spend budget caps.
- Replay and integrity verification.
- Human approval provenance.

## CTA library
- Explore a real decision.
- Book an AI Desk Audit.
- Apply as a design partner.
- Review the decision-evidence specification.

## Forbidden framing
Trading bot, guaranteed performance, passive income, effortless automation, winning signals, autonomous profits.

## Content review checklist
Every claim must answer: Is it accurate? Is it current? Is it public-safe? Is the evidence linked? Could a reasonable buyer mistake it for a performance promise?
