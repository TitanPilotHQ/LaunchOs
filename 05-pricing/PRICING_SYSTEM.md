# Titan Pilot Pricing System

## Principles
Price the governed desk, not signals or trades. Keep model spend transparent. Never tie fees to returns. No free pilots.

## Offer ladder
| Offer | Buyer | Price hypothesis | Purpose |
|---|---|---:|---|
| AI Desk Audit | Qualifying desk | $7,500 fixed | Paid diagnostic and committee-ready gap report |
| Design Partner | First five desks | $2,500/month, 6 months | Referenceable co-development |
| TITAN Desk | 5–20 seat desk | $4,000/month | Single-tenant supervised AI decision system |
| Enterprise | Multi-desk firm/fund | $120k floor; $150k–$250k typical | SSO, HA, VPC/on-prem, escrow, support |
| Prop Platform | Funded-trader operator | $60k/year + usage | Fleet governance |
| OEM | Platform vendor | $250k/year floor + royalty | Embedded decision evidence |
| TITAN Certified | Qualified deployments | $25k–$50k/year | Conformance and certification |

## Packaging rules
- First three symbol sets included in Desk; extra sets priced separately.
- LLM/API usage passed through at cost with hard limits.
- Customer-specific implementation is separately scoped.
- Services should remain below 30% of revenue by year two.

## Discount framework
Discount only for annual prepay, longer commitment, referenceability, reduced scope, or strategic distribution. Record the exchanged value. Never offer indefinite founder discounts.

## Renewal system
Renewal proof includes annual governance report, decision volume, committee usage, incident handling, prompt/version history, support outcomes, and roadmap fit.

## Metrics
ACV, gross margin, discount rate, implementation margin, time-to-value, audit-to-subscription conversion, renewal, expansion, concentration, services mix.

## Definition of done
A quote must state scope, exclusions, model spend, deployment boundary, retention, support, data ownership, liability posture, and success criteria.
