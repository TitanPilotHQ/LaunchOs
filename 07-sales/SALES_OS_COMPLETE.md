# Titan Pilot Sales Operating System

## Ideal customer qualification
A qualified prospect has: at least five traders, existing AI usage, and a risk committee/regulator to answer to. Three of three qualifies for Desk. Two of three qualifies for the paid audit. Anything less is nurture or disqualify.

## Pipeline stages
1. Named account researched.
2. Evidence-led outreach sent.
3. Discovery complete.
4. Live demo delivered.
5. AI Desk Audit proposed.
6. Audit paid and delivered.
7. Design partner/subscription proposed.
8. Security/legal review.
9. Closed won/lost.
10. Expansion/renewal.

## Discovery questions
- Where is AI used today?
- Which decisions does it influence?
- What evidence is retained?
- Who can approve or override?
- What does the risk committee ask?
- Who owns API cost and model choice?
- What incident would create personal or regulatory exposure?
- What systems must integrate?

## Demo-to-close motion
Use real sanitized evidence. Show one caught fabrication, one veto, one replay, one budget proof. Ask for the paid audit, not a vague next meeting.

## Outreach template
Subject: Can your desk reconstruct what AI told a trader last Tuesday?

Your traders are likely already using AI. Most desks can show a policy but not a decision record. Titan Pilot records cited analysis, objections, deterministic controls, approvals, refusals, and cost as replayable evidence. We offer a paid two-week AI Desk Audit to show the exact governance gap.

## Disqualification rules
No retail signal seekers. No returns-first buyer. No free pilot. No gray broker. No buyer unwilling to retain an audit trail.

## CRM fields
Segment, jurisdiction, desk size, AI usage, committee owner, pain, current evidence, integration, legal blockers, next step, amount, probability, loss reason.

## Sales KPIs
Named accounts, qualified discovery rate, demo-to-audit conversion, audit-to-subscription conversion, sales cycle, ACV, discount, win/loss reason, referenceability.

## Definition of done
A deal is closed only when commercial terms, data boundary, implementation scope, evidence rights, named owner, success criteria, and first governance review date are agreed.
