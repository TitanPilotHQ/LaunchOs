# Titan Pilot Investor Data Room

## Purpose
Provide a staged, evidence-backed diligence environment. No secrets, broker identifiers, customer strategy data, or unsupported claims.

## Folder map
### 01 Company
Incorporation, ownership, cap table, governance, founder profile, hiring plan.

### 02 Product
Product thesis, Phase D design, roadmap, demo guide, public instance.

### 03 Technology
Engineering handbook, ADR summary, architecture diagrams, release history, test posture.

### 04 Reliability and security
Phase C/C.5 certifications, security audit, DR/PITR report, incident register, dependency posture.

### 05 Market
ICP, category thesis, bottom-up market sizing, buyer interviews, regulatory research.

### 06 Commercial
Pricing, pipeline, audits, design partners, references, customer health.

### 07 Competition and moat
Competitor matrix, evidence-capability benchmark, switching-cost thesis, open-spec strategy.

### 08 Finance
P&L, cash, runway, ARR/MRR, gross margin, model spend, forecast scenarios.

### 09 Legal
IP assignment, trademarks, privacy, terms, insurance, counsel memos.

### 10 Evidence
Public claim register, benchmark methods, research reports, corrections.

## Access tiers
- Teaser: public-safe overview.
- Qualified: product, market, selected evidence.
- Diligence: finance, legal, security summaries.
- Final: full controlled room after NDA.

## Data-room controls
Watermark sensitive files, log access, set expiration, review quarterly, and maintain one canonical version per artifact.

## Investor FAQ minimum
Why now? Why this buyer? Why not returns? Why not a model-vendor feature? Moat? Liability? Solo-founder risk? Regulatory timing? Expansion? Exit options?

## Definition of done
Every investor claim has a source, every metric reconciles to finance, every technical statement matches Titan, and stale artifacts are removed rather than left ambiguous.
