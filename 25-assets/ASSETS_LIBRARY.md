# Titan Pilot Assets Library

## Asset classes
Logos, icons, diagrams, screenshots, public decision fixtures, reports, decks, one-pagers, social crops, video masters, audio, legal-approved copy, and templates.

## Required metadata
Asset ID, title, owner, source, license, public/private classification, created date, review date, checksum, canonical path, derivative list, linked claim IDs, and intended channels.

## Folder standard
- `brand/` master logos and identity assets.
- `product/` approved screenshots and diagrams.
- `evidence/` sanitized decision fixtures and charts.
- `investor/` deck and data-room derivatives.
- `sales/` one-pagers, proposals, demo assets.
- `social/` channel-sized derivatives.
- `video/` source, edit, captions, thumbnails.
- `archive/` released or superseded versions.

## Safety rules
Never store secrets, customer-identifying records, private broker/account data, unlicensed media, or raw internal payloads in the public repository. Screenshots require a public-safety review.

## Versioning
Master assets are immutable after release. Corrections create a new version and archive the old one. Publicly distributed files include version/date metadata where practical.

## Quality checklist
Correct logo, legible at target size, accessible contrast, accurate product state, no unsupported metric, no stale status, correct rights, optimized file size, descriptive alt text.

## Definition of done
An asset is approved only when its metadata is complete, its source and rights are known, public claims are registered, and all derivatives trace back to one canonical master.
