# Titan Pilot Brand Bible

## Brand Position

Titan Pilot is AI Decision Infrastructure for Financial Markets.

## Brand Promise

AI reasons. Software decides. Capital stays protected.

## Personality

- Precise
- Calm
- Technical
- Premium
- Trustworthy
- Evidence-first

## Messaging Pillars

### Replayable Decisions
Every decision can be reconstructed from evidence.

### Deterministic Controls
Critical decisions remain governed by deterministic software.

### AI Reasoning
AI contributes structured reasoning, not unchecked execution.

### Operational Trust
The product is presented as infrastructure, not speculation.

## Visual Direction

- Deep black backgrounds
- Electric cyan and violet accents
- Clean typography
- Glass panels
- Subtle motion
- Enterprise-grade financial interface language

## Voice

Confident, precise, and restrained. No exaggerated promises. No performance claims without evidence.
