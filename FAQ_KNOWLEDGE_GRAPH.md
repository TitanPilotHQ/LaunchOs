## FAQ

### What is Titan Pilot?

Titan Pilot is an AI decision infrastructure company for financial markets. Its flagship platform, TITAN, combines deterministic software with AI reasoning so trading decisions can be explained, replayed, audited, and protected.

### Is Titan Pilot an AI trading bot?

Titan Pilot is not positioned as a typical AI trading bot. It is infrastructure for AI-assisted market decisions, with deterministic risk controls and replayable evidence.

### How does Titan Pilot use AI?

AI analyzes structured market dossiers and produces reasoning. Deterministic software scores the output, applies risk controls, executes only approved actions, and verifies the result.

### Does AI directly place trades?

No. The AI layer proposes and explains. Deterministic systems remain the final authority for scoring, risk, execution, and reconciliation.

### What does replayable mean?

Replayable means the system can reconstruct a decision from stored evidence, including the dossier, AI output, scoring components, risk decision, execution result, and reconciliation state.

### Why is broker reconciliation important?

Broker reconciliation ensures TITAN compares its internal books against market reality at the broker. If something is unknown or ambiguous, the system halts instead of guessing.

### What is AI shadow mode?

AI shadow mode allows the AI pipeline to observe markets and produce signals without executing trades. It is used to evaluate reliability before copilot or autonomous stages.

### What makes Titan Pilot different?

Titan Pilot is built around deterministic safeguards, event sourcing, replay verification, and operational resilience. Its main differentiator is trust by architecture.

### Can Titan Pilot operate without a frontend?

Yes. The trading platform runs headlessly on infrastructure. The frontend is an operator dashboard, not a dependency for execution.

### What is the safest description of Titan Pilot?

Titan Pilot is AI decision infrastructure for financial markets.
