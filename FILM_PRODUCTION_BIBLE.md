# Titan Pilot Film Production Bible

## Purpose

Create cinematic launch films for Titan Pilot that position the company as serious AI infrastructure for financial markets.

## Film System

Create one shared visual asset library that can generate:

- 90 second investor film
- 45 second homepage hero film
- 30 second social ad
- 15 second teaser
- animated website loops

## Creative Direction

The films should feel like a blend of enterprise AI, aerospace software, financial infrastructure, and premium product design.

Avoid retail trading visuals, gambling energy, fake wealth imagery, and unrealistic profit claims.

## Core Film Message

Markets do not need more guessing.

They need decisions that can be explained, replayed, audited, verified, and protected.

## Visual Language

- dark cinematic atmosphere
- glowing market data streams
- AI neural topology
- clean financial dashboards
- event timelines
- risk gates
- replay reconstruction
- calm but powerful motion

## 90 Second Film Structure

### Scene 1 — The Market Never Sleeps
Fast moving financial data, global market lights, quiet tension.

### Scene 2 — The Problem
Opaque automation, emotion, noise, fragmented decision-making.

### Scene 3 — Titan Pilot Appears
Logo reveal with calm confidence.

### Scene 4 — How Titan Thinks
Market dossier, AI analyst, devil's advocate, scorer, risk gate.

### Scene 5 — Trust Architecture
Immutable event log, replay, reconciliation, verification.

### Scene 6 — Recovery
A system fails, recovers, verifies, and continues safely.

### Scene 7 — The Future
Autonomous intelligence engineered for markets.

## Voice Direction

Low, calm, cinematic, confident. No hype.

## Suggested Closing Line

Titan Pilot. Autonomous intelligence. Engineered for markets.

## Google Veo Prompt Style

Use precise cinematic prompts with lens, lighting, mood, movement, material, color, and duration.

Every shot should be designed as part of one coherent film, not isolated clips.
