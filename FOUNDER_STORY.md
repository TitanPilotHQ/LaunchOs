# Founder Story

Titan Pilot began with a simple observation: modern AI can generate convincing answers, but financial systems require verifiable decisions.

The project started by solving engineering problems before AI problems—building replayability, deterministic risk controls, broker reconciliation, recovery, operational hardening, and continuous verification first.

Only after that foundation was proven did AI become part of the architecture.

## Principles

- Build trust before autonomy.
- Prefer evidence over claims.
- Fail closed instead of guessing.
- Every important decision should be explainable.
- Infrastructure is the product.

## Long-Term Vision

Create a platform where autonomous financial intelligence can operate within deterministic safety boundaries and produce decisions that are explainable, replayable, and auditable.
