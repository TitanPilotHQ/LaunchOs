# Titan Pilot Launch Campaign

## Campaign Goal

Launch Titan Pilot as a serious AI infrastructure company for financial markets, not as a retail trading bot.

## Campaign Theme

**AI reasons. Software decides.**

## Campaign Phases

### Phase 1 — Foundation

Prepare public assets:

- website
- brand bible
- investor kit
- launch film
- social profiles
- press kit
- product narrative

### Phase 2 — Education

Publish content that explains the engineering philosophy:

- Why AI trading systems need replayability
- Why LLMs should not directly execute trades
- Why deterministic risk gates matter
- How event sourcing improves auditability
- How broker reconciliation protects truth

### Phase 3 — Reveal

Release the public website and cinematic launch film.

Messaging focus:

- autonomous intelligence
- deterministic safety
- replayable decisions
- capital protection
- enterprise reliability

### Phase 4 — Proof

Show evidence instead of claims:

- architecture walkthrough
- replay timeline
- infrastructure validation story
- AI shadow mode report
- operational hardening summary

### Phase 5 — Investor Outreach

Use the investor kit, demo script, launch film, and technical narrative to approach selected investors, fintech operators, and technical partners.
