# Titan Pilot Narrative

## Core Belief

Financial AI needs trust by architecture.

## Why Titan Pilot Exists

Titan Pilot exists to build AI decision infrastructure for financial markets.

The goal is not to let AI act without boundaries. The goal is to combine structured AI reasoning with deterministic controls, replay, auditability, and recovery.

## Philosophy

AI reasons.

Software decides.

Risk remains deterministic.

Every decision becomes evidence.

## Category

Titan Pilot is AI Decision Infrastructure for Financial Markets.

## Product Story

TITAN turns market context into a structured dossier, asks AI agents to reason over that evidence, scores the output using deterministic rules, applies risk gates, records every step, and keeps the system verifiable through replay.

## Durable Advantage

The durable advantage is not one model. Models change.

The durable advantage is the system around the model:

- event spine
- replay engine
- deterministic scoring
- broker reconciliation
- operational recovery
- continuous verification

## Brand Promise

Trust by architecture.
