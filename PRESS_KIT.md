# Titan Pilot Press Kit

## Company Boilerplate

Titan Pilot is an AI decision infrastructure company for financial markets. The company is building a deterministic, replayable, and auditable platform where AI produces structured reasoning while software-controlled risk, execution, reconciliation, and verification remain the final authority.

Titan Pilot's flagship platform, TITAN, is designed around a simple principle: AI reasons, software decides.

## Short Description

Titan Pilot builds AI decision infrastructure for financial markets.

## Medium Description

Titan Pilot is developing TITAN, a financial AI infrastructure platform that combines deterministic software engineering, event-sourced audit trails, broker reconciliation, replay validation, and AI-assisted market reasoning.

## Long Description

Titan Pilot is building a new class of AI decision infrastructure for financial markets. Unlike conventional trading bots or signal services, TITAN separates AI reasoning from execution authority. AI agents can analyze structured market dossiers and propose signals, but deterministic software controls scoring, risk gates, execution, broker reconciliation, replay verification, and operational safety.

The platform is designed for explainability, auditability, reproducibility, and capital protection.

## Founder Quote

"Markets do not need more black-box automation. They need intelligent systems whose decisions can be explained, replayed, audited, and protected. Titan Pilot is built around that principle."
