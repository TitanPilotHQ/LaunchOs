# Titan Pilot Product Demo Script

## Demo Goal

Show Titan Pilot as a trustworthy AI decision infrastructure platform, not as a speculative trading tool.

## Demo Audience

- investors
- technical partners
- fintech leaders
- engineering buyers
- AI infrastructure operators

## Demo Flow

### 1. Opening

"Titan Pilot is AI decision infrastructure for financial markets. The platform is designed so every decision can be explained, replayed, audited, and protected before a trade is ever placed."

### 2. System Health

Show the operator view:

- services online
- broker connected
- reconciliation ok
- database ok
- replay verification ok
- backup ok
- AI status

Message: the system knows its own state before it makes decisions.

### 3. Market Dossier

Show a generated market dossier:

- H4 context
- H1 decision timeframe
- M15 timing
- cost and spread
- session context
- macro status

Message: AI never receives raw chaos. It receives structured evidence.

### 4. AI Reasoning

Show the analyst thesis and Devil's Advocate response.

Message: the system does not rely on one unchallenged model opinion.

### 5. Deterministic Score

Show score breakdown:

- trend
- structure
- momentum
- volatility
- macro
- cost
- objections

Message: software scores the decision. LLM confidence is only advisory.

### 6. Risk Gate

Show deterministic risk approval or rejection.

Message: AI proposes; risk decides.

### 7. Replay

Replay one decision:

Market data → dossier → AI thesis → objections → score → risk → execution → broker confirmation → reconciliation

Message: every decision becomes evidence.

### 8. Recovery

Explain crash recovery and broker reconciliation.

Message: the system does not guess market truth. The broker is authoritative.

### 9. Closing

"Titan Pilot is not built around prediction hype. It is built around trust, verification, and capital protection."

## Demo Rules

- Do not show unverified performance claims.
- Do not imply live-money profitability.
- Emphasize system behavior, not profit promises.
- Use real engineering evidence where possible.

## Ideal Demo Length

7 to 10 minutes.
