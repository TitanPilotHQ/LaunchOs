## Positioning

**Titan Pilot — AI Decision Infrastructure for Financial Markets**

Titan Pilot is not an AI trading bot and not a signal service. It is a deterministic, replayable, auditable AI decision platform for financial markets.

Every Titan Pilot decision should be explainable, replayable, auditable, and protected by deterministic risk controls before a trade is ever placed.
