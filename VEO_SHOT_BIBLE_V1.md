# Titan Pilot Veo Shot Bible v1

## Purpose

Define a cinematic shot system for Titan Pilot launch videos using Google Veo or equivalent generative video tools.

## Production Principle

Do not create random clips. Create a coherent film language that can be reused across the website, investor film, LinkedIn, and launch trailers.

## Master Film Tone

- cinematic
- dark premium technology
- calm intensity
- financial infrastructure
- AI intelligence without gimmicks
- enterprise credibility

## Color Grade

Deep black, cool blue, cyan highlights, violet AI glow, restrained contrast.

## Film 1 — 90 Second Investor Film

### Scene 1 — Markets Never Sleep

Visual: global financial centers at night, abstract market data moving through dark networks.

Camera: slow aerial push, cinematic lens, shallow depth.

Voice: "Markets never sleep. Decisions happen faster than people can react."

### Scene 2 — The Problem

Visual: fragmented dashboards, noisy signals, unstable automation, charts flashing but no clear decision path.

Voice: "Most systems still ask traders to trust what they cannot replay."

### Scene 3 — Titan Pilot Reveal

Visual: dark screen, cyan light forming the Titan Pilot mark, clean interface surfaces emerging.

Voice: "Titan Pilot is built differently."

### Scene 4 — Market Dossier

Visual: raw market streams compress into a structured dossier panel with H4, H1, and M15 blocks.

Voice: "Market context becomes structured evidence."

### Scene 5 — AI Reasoning

Visual: AI analyst panel, thesis card, Devil's Advocate objections, deterministic score card.

Voice: "AI reasons over evidence. It does not control execution."

### Scene 6 — Risk Gate

Visual: glowing gate with deterministic checks passing or blocking.

Voice: "Software decides what is allowed to move forward."

### Scene 7 — Execution and Broker Truth

Visual: order intent travels through secure rails to broker confirmation, then returns as verified evidence.

Voice: "Broker truth is reconciled. Nothing is guessed."

### Scene 8 — Replay

Visual: entire decision reconstructs backward as an event timeline.

Voice: "Every decision can be replayed. Every step can be audited."

### Scene 9 — Recovery

Visual: system interruption, fail-closed pause, recovery, green verification state.

Voice: "When uncertainty appears, trading reduces to zero."

### Scene 10 — Closing

Visual: Titan Pilot logo over a calm mission-control dashboard.

Voice: "Titan Pilot. Autonomous intelligence. Engineered for markets."

## 45 Second Homepage Hero Cut

Use scenes 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 10.

## 30 Second Social Ad

Focus on problem, dossier, AI reasoning, replay, closing logo.

## 15 Second Teaser

Markets never sleep. Titan Pilot does not guess.

## Prompt Template

Create a cinematic shot of [subject], dark premium AI infrastructure aesthetic, deep black background, cyan and violet light accents, precise glass UI panels, financial data streams, calm enterprise tone, slow camera movement, high contrast, realistic lighting, no cartoon style, no retail trading hype.

## Negative Prompt Guidance

Avoid gambling imagery, luxury cars, cash piles, unrealistic profit charts, chaotic neon overload, cartoon robots, fake celebrity traders, and exaggerated wealth visuals.
